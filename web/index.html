About IEEE


The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Inc. (IEEE) was founded in 1884 with Alexander Graham Bell and Thomas Edison among its charter members. IEEE, an association dedicated to advancing innovation and technological excellence for the benefit of humanity, is the world's largest technical professional society. It is designed to serve professionals involved in all aspects of the electrical, electronic, and computing fields and related areas of science and technology that underlie modern civilization. Today, IEEE serves over 422,000 members, geographically organized into 10 regions in over 160 countries. Over 319 sections all over the world and more than 1,676 chapters unite the local IEEE with members with similar technical interests. There are more than 80 Women in Engineering Student Branch Affinity groups worldwide. Over 80,000 student members world-wide make up 22% of the membership of the IEEE and are essential to the continued growth and vitality of the IEEE. Over 50% of the current IEEE members joined as students. Not only is the IEEE the world's largest technical and professional society, it also publishes 30% of the literature in the world with publishing more than 100 peer-reviewed journals. There are over 900 active IEEE standards at present and more than 400 are in development by IEEE. IEEE's worldwide membership is geographically divided into ten regions. These regions are further divided into sections that serve as the centers of activity for professional engineers at the local level. A student branch falls under the auspices of its local IEEE section.


IEEE National Institute of Technology-Agartala Student Branch


IEEE National Institute of Technology-Agartala Student Branch(IEEE NIT-A SB) is a student branch having SB code number STB11469 and School Code 60052631 is registered under the Kolkata Section. Currently, the SB have more than 50 student members. It covers Professional Awareness Activities, Branch Operations, Branch Administration, Membership, Fundraising and detailed description of the awards and achievements for students which is for the advancement of technology for humanity. The NIT-A Student Branch is operated by Institute Level Committee (ILC) as well as by the committee mentioned below.


Vision


IEEE NIT Agartala Student Branch is committed to create a better world with sustained development through potential professional and social platform leading to innovation quest and responsible citizens.


Mission


1. To create technical awareness among the students with the latest innovations and technological advancements.
2. To aware the students about outcome-oriented learning and innovative research ideas through seminar and workshops.
3. To create awareness regarding cutting-edge industrial technologies and practices by conducting industrial tours.
4. Guidance from academic as well as industry experts for better career opportunities with professional competencies.
5. To make them acquainted with various environmental and social responsibilities.
